Pro Trading Tips

Combine Multiple Indicators

Don't rely on a single indicator. Use trend indicators (MACD, EMA) with momentum indicators (RSI) for confirmation and better entry points.

RSI Divergence Strategy

Watch for divergence between RSI and price. If price makes a new high but RSI doesn't, it signals potential reversal.

Bollinger Band Squeeze

When bands narrow significantly, it indicates low volatility and often precedes a major price move (breakout).

MACD Signal Line Crossover

Bullish signal: MACD line crosses above signal line. Bearish signal: MACD line crosses below signal line.

Choose The Right Timeframe

Day traders: 5-15 min charts. Swing traders: 1-4 hour charts. Position traders: Daily/Weekly charts.
